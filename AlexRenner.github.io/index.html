Though Mr. Holland's Opus recieved favorable audience reviews when it was first released, it still was not critically aclaimed at the time. It grossed over $100,000,000 worldwide and resulted in the sound track's composer's, Michael Kamen, opening of the Mr. Holland's Opus Foundation (MHOF) which is dedicated to the future of music education.

Major actors in the film include:

  • Richard Dreyfuss as Glenn Holland
  • Glenne Headly as Iris Holland
  • Jay Thomas as Coach Bill Meister
  • Olympia Dukakis as initial Principal Helen Jacobs
  • William H. Macy as later Principal Gene Wolters
  • And many other spectacular cast members not listed here!
